good deeds

what we do

People want to volunteer now more than ever but knowing where and how to give your time and skills isn’t always clear. We strive to create a platform for volunteers from all background and skill levels to connect with local non-profits that need help.

Once you are registered with an account, we take care of matching you with an ideal non-profit partner to arrange a volunteer session.

There’s always good work that needs to be done. We want to help make it happen.
